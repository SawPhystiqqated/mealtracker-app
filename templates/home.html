Welcome to NutriLogic

NutriLogic is a nutrition tracking and decision-support application designed to help users become more aware of their eating habits, meal patterns, and dietary needs.

The application allows you to log meals, review your intake history, and make informed food choices based on personal preferences and health goals.

Whether you're managing dietary restrictions, building healthier habits, or simply staying mindful of nutrition, NutriLogic is designed to support you.
